---

## File: `collaboration_policy.md`

### Purpose

To define **how external researchers, students, and institutions may collaborate with AIC-research**.

### Accepted Collaboration Types

* Joint research exploration
* Independent replication attempts
* Critical review and formal rebuttal
* Student research projects (with clear supervision)

### Requirements

* Alignment with research principles
* Willingness to document assumptions and failures
* Respect for security and dual-use constraints

### Explicit Boundaries

* No data-sharing involving sensitive or personal data
* No pressure toward premature deployment
* No co-branding without substantive contribution

### Attribution

* Contributions are credited by work, not by affiliation

---

## Closing Note

Academic relations in AIC-research are built on:

* Shared responsibility
* Intellectual honesty
* Willingness to disagree rigorously

Collaboration is welcomed, but never at the cost of clarity, safety, or accountability.
